The Edo Malaria Density Application

About

The Edo Malaria Density Application uses color codes to show the density of malaria cases across the 18 Local Government Areas in Edo state in the year 2012.

These visualizations utilize data collected in 2012 and published on the Edo State Open Data Portal.
